About Us

About Rapid Crash Repairs

Serving the Cavan community for over 15 years, Rapid Crash Repairs has built a reputation as the most trusted collision repair specialist in the region. Located on Dublin Road in Drumlion, we've helped thousands of drivers get back on the road safely after accidents. Our team combines traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver exceptional results every time.

We work directly with all major insurance companies, making the claims process as smooth as possible for our customers. From minor dents to major collision repairs, our fully equipped workshop handles everything with professionalism and care.

"Rapid Crash Repairs did an amazing job on my car after a bad accident. You'd never know it was damaged - looks better than new! The team kept me updated throughout and handled everything with the insurance company. Highly recommended." Sarah Murphy Satisfied Customer
